Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-24

Reading Log ("we", "our") helps users record and manage their reading history. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Account information (via social login): We may receive basic profile information such as your email address and a user identifier from your chosen login provider (e.g., Google, Apple) to authenticate you.
  • User content: Reading records you create in the app (e.g., book titles, notes) may be stored to provide the service and sync across devices.
  • Diagnostics (optional): If enabled, we may collect crash logs or performance data to improve stability.

How We Use Data

  • To provide login and account access.
  • To store and sync your reading records across devices.
  • To maintain and improve the app (e.g., troubleshooting, bug fixes).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., authentication or hosting), and only for the purposes described above.

Data Retention

We retain your account and reading records while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion, email sdgb.union@gmail.com with the subject Account deletion request and include the email used to sign in.

Security

We use standard security measures and encrypted connections (e.g., HTTPS) when communicating with our servers.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at sdgb.union@gmail.com.
